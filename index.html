Secure & Private Browsers

Why Use Secure Browsers?

Secure browsers protect your privacy by blocking trackers, preventing fingerprinting, and not storing your browsing history.

Browser Security Tips

Use Privacy Extensions

Install uBlock Origin, Privacy Badger, HTTPS Everywhere

Block Third-Party Cookies

Disable cookies from tracking websites

Use Private Mode

Browse in incognito/private mode when needed

Keep Updated

Always use the latest browser version
